Project directed by *Don Kramer* and *Daniel Donnecke*, coded by *Steffi LaZerte*

This is the data exploration and analysis of Turkey Vulture kettling and migration behaviour
above Rocky Point on southern Vancouver Island. 

Published in the Journal of Raptor Research

Dönnecke, D., LaZerte, S. E., & Kramer, D. L. (2026). Turkey Vulture Aggregations at a Water Barrier Provide Evidence of Later Migration and Increasing Population Size over 25 Years. Journal of Raptor Research (published online ahead of print 2026). https://doi.org/10.3356/jrr2491
